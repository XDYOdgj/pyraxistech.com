Black Diamond Stoneworks Stone & Tile Intensive Cleaner

Keep your natural stone and tile surfaces sparkling clean with the reliable performance of Black Diamond Stoneworks Stone & Tile Intensive Cleaner. This powerful formula is designed to tackle tough stains and grime while being gentle enough to protect the delicate finish of your floors. With its non-abrasive, pH-neutral composition, it ensures that your home stays clean and beautiful without compromising the integrity of your surfaces.

What Makes It Special?

Whether you're maintaining a sleek marble kitchen countertop, a smooth limestone bathroom floor, or a charming travertine patio, this cleaner delivers consistent, professional results. Its concentrated 1-quart formulation creates up to 8 gallons of cleaning solution, making it perfect for both everyday use and large-scale applications. Built with eco-friendly principles and American craftsmanship, it's trusted by homeowners and professionals alike for superior care of your stone and tile surfaces.

Black Diamond Stoneworks offers a versatile solution that works on a wide range of surfaces, including polished and honed natural stone and ceramic tile. Its eco-conscious design ensures you're making a responsible choice without sacrificing quality or effectiveness.
